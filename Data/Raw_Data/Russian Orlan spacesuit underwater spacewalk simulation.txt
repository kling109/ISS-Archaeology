<b>Description:</b>  Attired in a Russian Orlan spacesuit, astronaut Michael E. Lopez-Alegria, Expedition 14 commander and NASA space station science officer, participates in an underwater spacewalk simulation in the Hydrolab facility at the Gagarin Cosmonaut Training Center in Star City, Russia. SCUBA-equipped divers are in the water to assist the crewmembers in their rehearsal, intended to help prepare them for work on the exterior of the station.
