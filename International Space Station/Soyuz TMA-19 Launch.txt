The Soyuz TMA-19 rocket launched from the Baikonur Cosmodrome in Kazakhstan June 15, 2010 at 5:35 p.m EDT. The mission carried Expedition 24 NASA astronauts Shannon Walker and Douglas Wheelock, and Soyuz Commander Fyodor Yurchikhin to the International Space Station.
