The Expedition One Crew poses for an informal portrait while in the Node 1/Unity module of the International Space Station (ISS). From left to right are: Yuri Gidzenko (soyuz vehicle commander), Sergei Krikalev (Flight Engineer), and Expedition 1 commander William M. Shepherd. Krikalev is holding parts to the Interim Resistive Exercise Device (IRED) which the crew assembled and used during exercise periods.
